About Me

Hi there! I’m Sajjad Afzal, a passionate tech enthusiast and a 4th-semester Computer Science student at Comsats University Lahore. My journey into the world of technology began with a curious twist—switching from a pre-medical background to engineering, and finally diving into the thrilling field of computer science.

I’ve always been fascinated by the power of knowledge. Whether it’s immersing myself in books, exploring cutting-edge technologies, or learning new tools, I’m fueled by a love for continuous growth. Currently, my tech stack includes JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Node.js, and I’m diving deeper into web development, DevOps, and cloud computing.

Beyond coding, I’m a sports enthusiast who’s equally at home on the cricket pitch, the football field, or the volleyball court. Sports have taught me teamwork, resilience, and discipline—skills I carry into every aspect of my life.
